How does hardware work?

In the "How Does Hardware Work project, my groupmates and I explored the basic principles of how hardware operates using logic gates. We designed and analyzed fundamental gates such as AND, NOT, OR, NAND, and XOR to understand how they process binary inputs and produce outputs. Through this project, we learned how these gates serve as the building blocks of digital circuits and modern computer systems. Working collaboratively also helped us improve our problem-solving and teamwork skills. Overall, this project strengthened our understanding of how simple components work together to perform complex hardware functions.
